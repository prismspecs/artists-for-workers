The most secure method of leaking sensitive information is via Secure Drop. We do not currently have a Secure Drop node ready.

As an alternative, we suggest the following:

  • register for free, encrypted ProtonMail
  • print any documents you wish to send -- this ensures that no metadata is embedded in a digital file sent to us
  • scan or take photos of those documents
  • to scrub any additional metadata introduced from a phone camera, etc. send the photo via Signal to a friend
  • download the photo that has been sent via Signal
  • send the documents to info@artistsforworkers.org
  • if you are unable to complete some of these tasks, know that we will scrub the data on our side as well and delete any originals to protect your safety
